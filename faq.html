Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is the event taking place?

August 20, 2023 5PM at the Waterfront Hotel Cebu City, Pacific Ballroom

What is the purpose or theme of the event?

The TFOE-PE, Inc. Visayas Convention is a dynamic midyear gathering that unites all Kuya from the Visayas region. This event serves as a catalyst for stronger unity and collaboration among participants. Through workshops, seminars, and engaging activities, each Kuya have the opportunity to exchange ideas, enhance their skills, and foster personal growth. The convention aims to empower each Kuya, reinforcing the organization's values of brotherhood, service, and humanitarianism. By fostering camaraderie and a sense of purpose, the Eagles Visayas Convention propels each Kuya to become catalysts of positive change in their communities and embodies the organization's commitment to service and collective impact.
